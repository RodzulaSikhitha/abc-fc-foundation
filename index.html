ABC FC Foundation

Football for Change

Founded in 2023, ABC FC Foundation channels the lion's spirit into real upliftment - creating opportunity, discipline and purpose for communities across Vhembe and beyond.

Community

Grassroots football bringing communities together in the Vhembe district.

Talent

Funding full SAB League participation - from trials to trophies.

Education

Skills, leadership and life development programs beyond the pitch.
