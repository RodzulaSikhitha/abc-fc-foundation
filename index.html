ABC FC Foundation (NPC)

Football for Change

Founded in 2023, ABC FC Foundation (NPC) channels the lion's spirit into real upliftment — creating opportunity, discipline and purpose for communities across Vhembe and beyond.

Community

Grassroots football bringing communities together in the Vhembe district.

Talent

Funding full SAB League participation — from trials to trophies.

Education

Skills, leadership and life development programs beyond the pitch.

Community Outreach

2018
Malaika Orphanage, Hillbrow

Team visit bringing support to children in Johannesburg.

2021
Jozi Homeless Christmas Feed

Sharing a Christmas meal with the less fortunate on the streets of Joburg.

2022
Soweto Old Age Home

Year-end visit to elderly residents in Soweto.

2024
Smile Orphanage Home

Bringing smiles and support to children at Smile Orphanage.

Hollywood Bets SAB League

The foundation funds the club's complete SAB League participation — registration, kit, transport, and coaching under SAFA Vhembe Region.

  • Full SAFA registration & compliance
  • Kit and equipment provision
  • Transport to all fixtures
  • Coaching and technical support
📚 Skills

ABC Grow Learning

Leadership, communication and entrepreneurship for players building lives beyond football.

  • Leadership workshops
  • Communication training
  • Entrepreneurship fundamentals

Social Outreach

Orphanage visits, old-age home engagement, and community feeding drives — because football is bigger than ninety minutes.

  • Orphanage visits
  • Homeless feeding drives
  • Youth mentorship

Open trials for players who carry the lion's spirit — Attitude, Belief, Courage. Structured evaluation, clear pathways.

  • Seasonal trial windows
  • Skills and 11v11 assessment
  • All positions considered

A Master of Public Administration graduate from the University of Pretoria and Group Head at the City of Johannesburg. Lucas founded ABC FC Foundation (NPC) in 2023 and chairs the African by Choice Group (Pty) Ltd across logistics, property, lodging and transport.
