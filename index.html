Who We Are

African by Choice — A Legacy Built on Purpose

ABC FC Foundation (NPC) was founded in May 2023 by Lucas Tshibalo Mudau, a senior government official and passionate advocate for community development through sport. The foundation operates as a registered Non-Profit Company with a clear mandate: to develop football talent and create pathways for young South Africans to succeed.

Based in Makwarela, Thohoyandou in the Vhembe District of Limpopo, the foundation funds the club's participation in the prestigious Hollywood Bets SAB League under the SAFA Vhembe Region — one of South Africa's most competitive regional football structures.

Our Foundation

The Power of Football to Change Lives

The ABC FC Foundation (NPC) channels the passion of football into tangible community impact. We believe that sport is one of the most powerful tools for social change — creating discipline, purpose, and opportunity for young people across South Africa.

Community Development

We fund and support grassroots football activities that bring communities together, focusing on the youth of Vhembe region and beyond.

Talent Development

Funding competitive football participation in the Hollywood Bets SAB League, giving talented players a platform to showcase their abilities.

Education & Growth

Beyond the pitch, we invest in the personal and professional growth of players — developing leaders and contributors to South African society.

What We Do

Programs & Initiatives

Football Development

Hollywood Bets SAB League

The ABC FC Foundation funds the club's full participation in the Hollywood Bets SAB League under SAFA Vhembe Region — providing players with structured, competitive football from registration to kit, transport and beyond.

  • Full registration and compliance support
  • Team kit and equipment provision
  • Transport to fixtures and tournaments
  • Coaching and technical support
Skills Development

ABC Grow Learning

A structured program that takes players beyond the pitch — developing leadership, communication, and entrepreneurial skills for life after football.

  • Visionary Leadership workshops
  • Communication and clarity training
  • Entrepreneurship fundamentals
  • Personal values alignment
Community

Social Outreach

Regular visits to orphanages, old-age homes, and community initiatives — because our responsibility extends far beyond ninety minutes on the pitch.

  • Orphanage visits and support
  • Elderly care home engagement
  • Homeless feeding drives
  • Youth mentorship programs
Opportunity

Player Trials

Open trials welcoming passionate players who embody Attitude, Belief and Courage. We offer structured evaluation across all positions with clear pathways into the squad.

  • Regular trial windows each season
  • Skills, fitness, and 11v11 assessment
  • Open to all positions
  • Transparent selection process

Founder & Chairman

Lucas Tshibalo Mudau, MPA

A Master of Public Administration graduate from the University of Pretoria and senior official at the City of Johannesburg, Lucas established ABC FC Foundation in 2023 with a vision to harness football as a vehicle for community transformation. He also chairs the African by Choice Group (Pty) Ltd — spanning logistics, property, transport, and hospitality.
