Community Impact

ABC FC Foundation

The ABC FC Foundation exists to uplift the Vhembe community through football developing young talent, creating economic opportunity, and building pride in Limpopo.

32 Clubs in 2026 tournament
2008
Club Founded
Lucas Tshibalo Mudau establishes ABC FC in Thohoyandou, Limpopo building a football club from the ground up in the SAFA Vhembe region.
2023
1st ABC Foundation Tournament
The inaugural community tournament launches, bringing clubs from across Vhembe together. Fundraising for player development and community outreach begins.
2024
2nd Tournament & Giraffe Sports Partnership
Second tournament successfully held at Phalama Ground. Giraffe Sports joins as official merchandise & apparel partner, providing sporting regalia for participants.
2025
Stream I Dominance Begins
ABC FC asserts total control of the SAFA Vhembe Hollywoodbets Regional League Stream I a 34W 3D 0L record in the 2025/26 season. R190,000 raised through three tournaments.
2026
4th Annual Tournament & Stream I Champions
TOP 32 knockout format at Phalama Ground. 105 points, 92 goals scored, 12 conceded. Gomora United (chairman Joe Seanego) confirmed as participating club. The Lion of the North roars loud.
