Competition History

League Record

From building the foundation to back-to-back Stream dominance — the Lion's rise through SAFA Vhembe.

  • 2025/26
    Stream I Champions SAFA Vhembe Singo Fuels Regional League · Stream I

    Stream I Champions

    The Lion of the North at its mightiest. An unbeaten season in the Singo Fuels Regional League — 34 wins, 3 draws, zero losses. A points tally that put the whole of Vhembe on notice. Promotion on the cards.

  • 2024/25
    Stream I2 Champions · Promoted SAFA Vhembe · Stream I2

    Stream I2 Champions

    Title claimed and promotion earned from Stream I2. The championship was won convincingly — ABC FC had proved it belonged at a higher level and earned the right to compete in Stream I the following season.

  • 2023/24
    SAFA Vhembe · Stream C1

    6th Place — Foundation Built

    The journey began here. 6th place in Stream C1 was more than a finishing position — it was a statement of intent. The culture, discipline, and identity of ABC FC were forged in this season. Everything since has been built on this ground.
