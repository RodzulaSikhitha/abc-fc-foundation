ABC FC Foundation

The ABC FC Foundation exists to uplift the Vhembe community through football developing young talent, creating economic opportunity and building pride in Limpopo.

2008
Club Founded

Lucas Tshibalo Mudau establishes ABC FC in Thohoyandou building a football club from the ground up in SAFA Vhembe.

2023
1st ABC Foundation Tournament

The inaugural community tournament launches at Makonde Stadium. Fundraising for player development and community outreach begins.

2024
2nd Tournament & Giraffe Sports Partnership

Second tournament at Makonde Stadium. Giraffe Sports joins as official merchandise partner, providing sporting regalia for all participants.

2025 – 2026
Stream I Champions & 4th Annual Tournament

34W 3D 0L · 105 points · 92 goals scored · 12 conceded. TOP 32 knockout tournament confirmed. R190,000 raised. Gomora United joins as partner club. The Lion of the North roars.
